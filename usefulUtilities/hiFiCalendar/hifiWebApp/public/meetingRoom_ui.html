Meeting Room Connector
         
                
Connect your virtual meeting rooms to your Google Calendar for easy space management.

            

            

            

            

                

                    This app links the meeting room schedules on your Google Calendar to meeting rooms in your virtual HQ, allowing you to see current and future meetings without leaving your headquarters.
                

                

                    We will have read-only access to your Google Calendars, and will parse the latest event information from the specific calendars that you choose to connect. 
                


                

                    After you connect your Calendars, the only data that will be temporarily stored is information regarding the next 2 meetings currently scheduled on that calendar. That data is permanently deleted when the events have concluded.
                

                

                    Only rerun this app if you need to connect your virtual meeting rooms to different Google Calendars.
                

                

                    Click "Sign In" below to begin linking your calendar to your HQ!
